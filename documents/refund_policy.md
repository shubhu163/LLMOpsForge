# Refund Policy

_Last updated: 2025-01-15_

## Overview

Acme Cloud offers a straightforward refund policy for all self-service
subscription plans. This document describes refund eligibility, timelines, and
the process for requesting a refund.

## Refund Period

Customers may request a full refund within **30 days** of the original purchase
date. Refund requests submitted after the 30-day window are not eligible for a
full refund but may qualify for prorated credit at Acme's discretion.

## Eligibility

- The refund applies to the most recent billing charge only.
- Monthly and annual plans are both eligible within the 30-day window.
- Usage-based overage charges are **non-refundable**.
- Accounts found in violation of the Acceptable Use Policy are not eligible.

## How Refunds Are Issued

Approved refunds are returned to the **original payment method** within **5 to 10
business days**. Acme does not issue refunds as account credit unless the
customer explicitly requests it.

## Annual Plan Refunds

For annual plans cancelled within the 30-day window, customers receive a full
refund. Annual plans cancelled after 30 days are eligible for a **prorated
refund** of the unused portion, minus any discount that was applied for
committing to the annual term.

## How to Request a Refund

1. Sign in to the billing portal at `billing.acmecloud.example`.
2. Open **Billing → Payment History**.
3. Select the charge and click **Request Refund**.
4. Choose a reason and submit.

Refund requests are typically reviewed within **2 business days**. You will
receive an email confirmation once the refund is approved.

## Contact

For refund questions, contact `support@acmecloud.example` or open a ticket from
the in-app help menu.
