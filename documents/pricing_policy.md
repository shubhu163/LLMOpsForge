# Pricing & Plans

_Last updated: 2025-01-20_

## Plans Overview

Acme Cloud offers three subscription tiers: **Starter**, **Pro**, and
**Enterprise**. All paid plans are billed in USD.

## Starter

- Price: **$0 per month** (free tier).
- Includes up to **3 projects** and **1 seat**.
- 10,000 API requests per month included.
- Community support only.

## Pro

- Price: **$49 per user per month**, billed monthly.
- Annual billing: **$490 per user per year** (equivalent to 2 months free).
- Includes **unlimited projects** and up to **25 seats**.
- 1,000,000 API requests per month included.
- Email support with a 1 business day response target.

## Enterprise

- **Custom pricing**; contact sales.
- Unlimited seats and projects.
- Dedicated support with a Service Level Agreement.
- Single Sign-On (SSO) and audit logging included.

## Overage Charges

API requests beyond the plan's included quota are billed at **$0.50 per 1,000
requests**. Overage is calculated at the end of each billing cycle and is
**non-refundable**.

## Discounts

- Annual billing on Pro saves the equivalent of **2 months** versus monthly.
- Registered non-profits receive a **30% discount** on the Pro plan.
- Educational institutions may apply for the free Pro tier via the EDU program.

## Currency and Taxes

All prices are listed in USD and are exclusive of applicable taxes. Taxes are
calculated at checkout based on the billing address.

## Plan Changes

Upgrades take effect **immediately** and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at
the **start of the next billing cycle**.
